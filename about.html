About

My name is Omar Khalid. I decided to become a certified personal trainer after years in the fitness industry. So why not use the skills I have to help my clients reach their goals. Personal training is my passion, to see someone achieve their goals as a results of my help gives me a great pride and satisfaction as a professional personal trainer.


I believe the key to success is by building a solid foundation in the beginning. Fitness provides us with the ability to enjoy a healthy lifestyle. The beauty is that you get to choose your own fitness adventures; and the options are endless. As a certified personal trainer, my role is to motivate, enable and make training a fun and rewarding experience. I love to see my clients achieve their goals through a tailored guided fitness program.


I care about my clients, and there's nothing more important to me than helping somebody go through an experience that makes them happy, confident, and strong. I understand that life can be hectic and it's often tough to find time to work out.


I will take the time to make sure that you're 100% satisfied and happy. Let me help you to break your bad habits and get on the road to a fitter life. My goal is to help you reach yours, so contact me for more information and lets chat about your fitness needs.
